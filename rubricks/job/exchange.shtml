Fulbright Scholar Program

Administering Organisation:

USIS Exchange Office

Description:

Post-doctoral research or lecturer grants. Kandidat nauk or Doktor nauk degree required.

Fields:

American studies, Ukranian studies, Political & Social sciences, International Relations, Legal Studies, Economics and Buisness, Pedagogy, Educational Administration, Fine Arts & Arts Administration

Duaration:

one or two semeters in USA

Applications available at

USIS, Ministry of Education, IEAC, ACCELS offices and IRF "Osvita" centers throughout Ukraine

Note:

Ukrainian universities can also invite American Fulbright scholars. Contact USIS for mre information

Программа администрируется:

USIS отдел экономическихи профессиональных обменов

Описание:

Гранты на проведение исследований или чтение лекций в университетах США предоставляются на один или два семестра особам, которые владеют степенью кандидата или доктора наук в соотвествующих отрослях.

Отрасли:

Американознавство, украинознавство, политология и социальные науки, международные отношения, правознавство, экономика и бизнес, педагогика, администрирование в образовании, искусство, администрирование в искусстве.

Длительность:

один или два семестра в США

Информацию и анкеты можно получить:

USIS, Министерство образования Украины, IEAC (центр международных обменов в отраслях науки и образования), офисы ACCELS (американский совет по сотрудничеству в отраслях образования и изучения языков) и центры "Освіта" международного фонда "Відродження".

Примечание:

Украинские ВУЗы могут пригласить американского препода

Junior Faculty Development Program

Administering Organisation:

USIS Exchange Office

Description:

One-year grants for university-level teachers. Audit courses, develop cirriculum and consult American scholars on teachin methods in the humanities and social sciencis. Applicants should be under age forty, have 2-8 years teaching expirience and be working or have completed there kandidat degree. This program focuses on teaching more than research.

Fields:

Buisness, Administration, Economics, Education Administration, Enviromental Studies, Journalism, Law, Library And Information Science, Political Science, Psychology, Public Policy, Public Administration, Sociology, Architecture And Urban Planning, Cultural Anthropology, History, Linguistics, Philosophy, Literature, American Studies.

Individual study in the US and testing programs TOEFL, SAT, GRE, GMAT

Administering Organisation:

ACTR/ACCELS, IEAC (Inernational Educational Advising Center

Description:

University catalogs, general college guides and other program information and applications available. For ETS testing contact International Educational Advising Center (Kiev T. Shevchenko University, Room 203); ACCELS, Reional "Osvita" centers.

Freedom Support Act Future Leaders Exchange (High School Exchange program)

Description:

Academic year of study in the U.S.A. for 9th and 10th form students.

Note:

To participate in this program, students must begin with taking pre-test for the Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL). These tests are administrated each autumn at regional educational centers.
