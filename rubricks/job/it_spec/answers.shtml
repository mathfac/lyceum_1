About myself

I graduated from Chernovtsy State University/Kiev National University, department of CS. My major is computer, intellectual systems and networks. My BS thesis is Generating of real time 3D images and MS thesis is internet server processing/ automation of public payment reception in Chernovtsy region. I started to work as a programmer 5 years ago. I participated in some large projects and in a number of smaller one’s during this period. Most of them are currently in use and very successful. I am now working in National company “Gas of Ukraine”/ Vuldi company. I’m responsible for database designing, application development and system administration. I’m looking for a job where my skills and experience will be realized.

How would you describe your personality (my great strength)?

    • I enjoy programming and I like  gain new knowledges.
    • I like team work and it’s easy for me to be a leader. I often organized people in the team and set the goals for them.
    • I can work well under pressure. Once we had to meet a deadline – I didn’t expect that I can work so hard day and night. It was very challenging and I am proud to say that I finished the application on time.
    • I’m a sociable person and I have successful experience in people training. Last summer I trained 26 people to become computer familiar with the system of public utilities payment.

Why are you leaving your present job?

I really like my current company and people I work with, but it isn’t a software development company and  I can apply my knowledge and skills better in software oriented company. Also, because of better opportunity in America to develope my computer   programming skills.

What did you like most/least about your last or current position?

I like the  people I'm working with  and the management style of my company. Also I like the fact that I have responsibililty for application development from designing to implementation.

I don’t like that sometimes I don’t have enough time for sports and other activities.

Why do you want to work for us?

Your company is  rapidly growing and I think I will have a great opportunity for professional career growth working for Newtech United. I’m looking for a job where I my skills and experience will be realized. I like to study new technologies and I find Newtech United one of the best companies  to realize my goal.

Why you are “brilliant” for our company?

My skills, experience and interests make me valuable for Newtech United. Also I have experience in full life-cycle of project including setting the goals, development of project, solving the different problems, testing and maintaining. I’m experienced in team development.

What do you find most attractive about this position? What seems least attractive?

    • I like to create non-trivial projects and I like being part of a team
    • It’s interesting to work within a growing-oriented organization, where my analytical skills, extensive experience and creativity might be utilized to their full potential
    • I will miss my friends and relatives in Ukraine, but I think I’ll find a lot of interesting people in your company and in US. Also I can communicate with friend by e-mail, so it’s not a big problem.

How long would you stay with us?

I’m interested in a career with Newtech United and I hope that we could have a lasting relationship
